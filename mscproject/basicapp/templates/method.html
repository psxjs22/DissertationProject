The Project

This project aims to see what method is best at teaching people how to identify fake news.

The Process

  1. You'll be asked to read and sign a consent form to ensure you agree to take part and you are aware how your data will be handled.
  2. We'll ask you to take a short quiz to get a baseline measure of how well you are able to identify fake news.
  3. You'll be asked to either complete a short tutorial, the tutorial will be run in a few different ways, but the information you recieve will be the same in each version.
  4. We'll then get you to take the quiz again and we will then use this information to see which strategy works the most effectively.
  5. Following the test, you will be provided with a short debriefing form, which you will be able to download.
