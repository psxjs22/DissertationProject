The Project

Participating in our study is easy and informative, you'll be asked to read the ethics briefing form and provide your consent to join the study.

Next, we'll gather some basic demographic information to better understand our participant group. After that, you'll take a quiz to assess your current ability to identify fake news. Don't worry, it's not a test!

We'll then introduce you to our innovative gamified solution, where you'll learn valuable skills through interactive quizzes and engaging content. Once you've completed the learning portion, you'll take the quiz again to see how much you've improved. And for those who are interested, there's an optional debriefing session, where we'll discuss the features that indicate the accuracy of news articles to enhance your understanding further.

Your valuable participation will help us in the fight against fake news and contribute to building a more informed digital society!

If you have any questions about the project, process, data handling or anything else please get in touch.
