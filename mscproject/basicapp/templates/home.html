Introduction

Welcome to our pilot study! In today's digital world, fake news is a serious concern that can influence how adults perceive information and make decisions. We are here to help you identify fake news and tackle misinformation with our innovative approach.

Our study uses gamification, which means we apply game elements to make learning engaging and effective. Through interactive quizzes and challenges, you'll gain valuable skills in spotting fake news like a pro!

We've created a user-friendly website where adults can participate in our study. You'll find all the information you need to make informed choices and contribute to our research.

By joining our pilot study, you'll be helping us understand how to best combat fake news for adults. Together, we can create a more informed society and empower individuals to be critical thinkers. Join us now and become a part of the fight against fake news!
